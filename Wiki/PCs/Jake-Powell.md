# Jake Powell

> "Rode into Calvert and rode out the next morning, and the Calvert problem was solved." Nobody agrees on exactly what happened in Calvert. Some think men died. Some think it never happened at all. Jake doesn't talk about it.

---

## Description

A drifter — lean, watchful, unhurried. He carries a creased photograph of a woman named [[Wiki/NPCs/Cassidy]], roughly his age. He doesn't know if he loves her, but he wants to find her and find out. His past includes at least one incident in Calvert that locals still whisper about.

---

## Background

Jake Powell is a drifter whose past includes at least one incident in Calvert that locals still whisper about. He carries a creased photograph of a woman named [[Wiki/NPCs/Cassidy]] — roughly his age. His Epic background vow to find her runs beneath every episodic story. Progress surfaces as rumors, a recognized name, or a trail that goes cold — never the focus of a session, but never entirely absent.
