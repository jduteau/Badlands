# Badlands Regions

The ten major regions of the Badlands, each with a distinct climate, culture, and flavor.

## Overview

| Region | Character | Primary Livelihood |
|---|---|---|
| [[Wiki/Locations/Tall-Pines]] | Lush, temperate forest | Hunting, fishing, trapping, forestry |
| [[Wiki/Locations/Costa-Dorada]] | Sunny, populous coast | Trade, entrepreneurship, gold prospecting |
| [[Wiki/Locations/Mil-Piedras]] | Stark canyon and butte country | Herding, ranching, farming, foraging |
| [[Wiki/Locations/Big-Nothing]] | Merciless desert | Nomadic survival |
| [[Wiki/Locations/Lithic-Mountains]] | Jagged, snowcapped peaks | Mining, self-reliant homesteading |
| [[Wiki/Locations/High-Plains]] | Wind-ruled high plateau | Wind-adapted farming, milling |
| [[Wiki/Locations/The-Swale]] | Flood-prone wetlands | River fishing, boat trade |
| [[Wiki/Locations/Sea-of-Grass]] | Endless prairie | Ranching, homesteading |
| [[Wiki/Locations/The-Big-Muddy]] | Great river corridor | River trade, travel, vice |
| [[Wiki/Locations/Back-East]] | Urbanized, civilized (unwelcomely so) | Finance, politics, exploitation |

## Relevance to Campaign

All ten regions are available as settings for Jake Powell's wandering. The campaign begins before any region has been visited. Update this section as the campaign develops.
