# Mae Sutter

Runs the Dusty Rose saloon in [[Wiki/Locations/Perdition]]. Somewhere in her forties. Auburn hair pinned up, sleeves rolled to the elbow. She's heard every story twice and believes about half of them.

---

## Description

A practical, capable woman who manages the Dusty Rose with calm authority. Pours honest whiskey — waters it less than most. Observes everything, comments selectively. Her eyes move around the room while she talks to you.

---

## Role

Primary information hub for Perdition. She told Jake about Eli Crane's character, suggested he approach Clara Harker rather than Old Man Harker, mentioned the boarding house, and gave him cover for approaching the ranch (he could say Mae sent him). Serves as a reliable, non-threatening source of local knowledge.

---

## PC Relationship

**[Session 1]** Served Jake whiskey and stew. Shared what she knew about Eli Crane — his habits, his age, his poverty relative to that silver buckle. Advised Jake to go through Clara, not Old Man Harker. Gave Jake social cover by letting him use her name at the ranch.

---

## What the PC Knows

- Runs the Dusty Rose
- Knew Eli Crane: harmless, young, drank too much, bad at cards except once; she liked him
- Suspects something more than cattle theft is going on
- Believes Blaine is decent but hamstrung by his history with Harker
- Knows Clara Harker by reputation: more sense than her father, less pride

---

## GM Truth

Mae sees most of what passes through Perdition. Whether she knows more than she's said is TBD. She is not involved in the Harker situation, but she is a reliable ear to the ground.

## History
