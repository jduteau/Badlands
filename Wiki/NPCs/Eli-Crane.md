# Eli Crane

A ranch hand at [[Wiki/Locations/Harker-Ranch]]. Twenty-two or twenty-three years old. Found dead approximately ten days before Session 1. His identity — and the location of his actual remains — is now in question.

---

## Description

Young. Harmless. Came into [[Wiki/Locations/Perdition]] every few weeks to drink too much, sing too loud, and lose money at cards. Once bought a round for the whole house when he won a hand — the only time [[Wiki/NPCs/Mae-Sutter]] ever saw him win. She says whoever cut his throat didn't do it for money; Eli never had two coins to rub together.

He slept in the corner bunk of the bunkhouse — military-neat blanket, **E.C.** carved into the frame with a pocket knife over many idle nights.

---

## Role

The murder victim at the center of [[Wiki/Threats/Harker-Ranch-Murder]]. His death (and the theft of 40 head of cattle) is what drew [[Wiki/NPCs/Tom-Blaine]]'s investigation and ultimately hired [[Wiki/PCs/Jake-Powell]]. His identity is now a key question.

---

## PC Relationship

Never met. Jake is investigating his death and the circumstances around it.

---

## What the PC Knows

- Ranch hand at [[Wiki/Locations/Harker-Ranch]]
- 22-23 years old; harmless; drank too much; bad at cards
- Supposedly found with throat cut in a gulch east of the ranch, ~10 days before Session 1
- Supposedly buried in Perdition's cemetery
- [[Wiki/NPCs/Old-Man-Harker]] took his death hard (possibly because Eli was the same age as Clara Harker)
- Rumors of "something there" between Eli and Clara — Mae wouldn't confirm

**Evidence recovered [Session 1]:**
- A hat band with **E.C.** scratched inside — found on skeleton in creek arroyo, ~1 day south of the ranch
- A silver belt buckle (longhorn steer design, quality craftsmanship) — found beside the same skeleton
- The silver buckle is inconsistent with Eli's stated poverty ("never had two coins to rub together")
