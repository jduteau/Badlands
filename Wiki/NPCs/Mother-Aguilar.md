# Mother Aguilar

Runs Aguilar's boarding house in [[Wiki/Locations/Perdition]]. Older woman with gray-streaked black hair pulled back tight and sharp eyes. Adobe two-story building on the main street.

---

## Description

No-nonsense proprietor of the cleanest beds in Perdition. Her rules are simple and not negotiable: no smoking in bed, no visitors after ten, privy is out back. She assesses guests quickly — drifter, armed, dusty — and decides within seconds whether they pass muster. She cooks a mean breakfast.

---

## Role

Provides lodging and meals in Perdition. Her observation at the end of Session 1 — "Perdition is quiet at night" — delivered as reassurance but carrying an edge of self-persuasion.
