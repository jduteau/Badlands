# Samuel Reeves

Ranch hand at [[Wiki/Locations/Harker-Ranch]]. One of two hands who remained after Eli Crane's death and the exodus of other workers. Returned from the range at the end of Session 2.

---

## Description

Not yet seen up close. Clara Harker vouched for him — "good man, both of them" — in context of explaining the ranch's remaining crew.

---

## Role

One of the two surviving ranch hands at the Harker spread. He and [[Wiki/NPCs/Hector-Garza]] have been working the range during a period of fear and instability that drove others off. Whether he knows anything about Eli Crane's murder is unknown.
