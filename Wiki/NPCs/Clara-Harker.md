# Clara Harker

Daughter of [[Wiki/NPCs/Old-Man-Harker]]. Mid-twenties. Dark hair pulled back. Work dress with sleeves rolled up. The one running the Harker Ranch now that her father is ill.

---

## Description

She met Jake on the porch with a rifle in her hands and steady eyes. Up close: tired, calloused hands, the easy confidence of someone who grew up on this land. She makes decisions quickly and doesn't second-guess them. She does the hiring; she does most everything around the ranch these days.

---

## Role

De facto operator of [[Wiki/Locations/Harker-Ranch]]. Key contact for the Eli Crane investigation — [[Wiki/NPCs/Mae-Sutter]] specifically recommended Jake approach Clara rather than Old Man Harker. She knew Eli Crane; she may know things about the ranch trouble that her father doesn't or won't share.

---

## PC Relationship

**[Session 1]** Clara accepted Jake's story (he mentioned Mae Sutter) and hired him as a hand — $1/day, meals, tack room bunk, one week to prove himself. Her warning: "If you're looking for easy work, keep riding." She went inside to manage her father when Jake arrived, suggesting this dynamic is routine.

**[Session 2]** Hosted Jake at lunch with her father and Maria. Confirmed the ranch's troubles openly: scattered cattle, spooked hands, Eli's death. Made clear she is the decision-maker. Told Jake to report strange things to her — not into town. Gave Jake permission to roam the land and learn it. She does not know Jake is also working for her father.

---

## What the PC Knows

- Runs the Harker Ranch; father is ill and difficult
- Hired Jake on a one-week trial ($1/day, meals, tack room)
- Knew Eli Crane personally; his death clearly affected her; doesn't talk about finding the body
- Confirmed the ranch troubles are real: scattered cattle, hands quitting out of fear, Eli dead
- She believes "whatever's happening out here, it isn't finished"
- She found Eli's body — the old man said not to press her about the details [Session 2]

---

## GM Truth

She is aware something is deeply wrong at the ranch. Whether she knows more than she's said about Eli or the cattle theft is TBD. She is not a suspect — she is a potential ally.

---

## Bond Status

No bond held.

## History
