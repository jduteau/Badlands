# Old Man Harker

Owner of [[Wiki/Locations/Harker-Ranch]]. Ill. Suspicious. Has a long-standing conflict with [[Wiki/NPCs/Tom-Blaine]] severe enough that Blaine cannot set foot on Harker land without a fight.

---

## Description

Not yet directly encountered. Known by reputation and through the voices of others: Mae Sutter (likely to run you off with a shotgun), Tom Blaine (they had words, years back), and Clara (not well; difficult). The voice from inside the house — calling for Clara when Jake arrived — was sharp and querulous.

---

## Role

The ranch owner. His illness has left Clara to run the operation. His conflict with Blaine is the political complication that required Blaine to hire an outside investigator. He took Eli Crane's death hard — possibly because Eli was the same age as Clara.

---

## PC Relationship

**[Session 1]** Not directly met. Heard only — voice from inside the house. Jake is established on the ranch as a hired hand; encounter likely in Session 2.
