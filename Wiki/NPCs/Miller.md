# Miller

Owns and runs Miller's Livery in [[Wiki/Locations/Perdition]]. Sixty if he's a day — bowlegged, sun-creased, hands like old saddle leather. Spent his whole life around animals and it shows.

---

## Description

A thick-necked man going bald on top with arms like fence posts. He assesses horses the same way he assesses men: practical, unhurried, and usually accurate. He built the tack room himself after the third time someone stole a customer's belongings. Sleeps in the loft above the stalls.

---

## Role

Liveryman of Perdition. Secondary information source — mentioned Blaine's habit of buying drinks to newcomers, which served as a heads-up. Keeps gear safe; knows who comes and goes in town.

---

## PC Relationship

**[Session 1]** Took in Wrangler for a dollar, brushed and fed and stabled her properly. Kept Jake's saddlebags locked. Warned Jake (as an afterthought) that Blaine buys strangers drinks and asks questions. Retrieved everything without incident when Jake left the next morning.
