# Mil Piedras

The landscape that Easterners picture when they romanticize the Badlands. A vast and wild expanse stretching horizon to horizon, riven by deep canyons and towering buttes, with rock formations that seem carved by a giant hand. Hot summers and cold winters drive much geologic activity; long droughts are broken by short, intense rains.

## Features

- Awe-inspiring rock formations
- Rough, red earth
- Wide, blue sky arching overhead
- Every texture of rock
- Well-tended herds of sheep and cattle
- Sturdy houses couched against the wind
- The taste of grit on the wind

## Inhabitants & Culture

Resources are not scarce but they are hard to find. Badlanders here must be creative and clever, typically combining herding, ranching, farming, and foraging to get by.

## Quest Starter

Everyone is quite certain that one particular rock formation didn't used to be there. How does such a thing happen? What do you fear it means? And why do you swear to find out the truth?
