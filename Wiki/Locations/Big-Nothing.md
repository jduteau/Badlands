# Big Nothing

## Overview

Sun-scoured sand crossed by precious few animals, supporting precious few plants. Virtually no rain falls across the year; what does fall tends to come all at once, causing floods rather than nurturing. Daytime temperatures could near-boil water; nights will freeze it. "Nothing" may be hyperbolic, but little beckons the average traveler here.

## Description

- Merciless sun
- Featureless stretches of sand
- Twisted and gnarled cacti
- Opportunistic buzzards circling and waiting
- Precious few oases
- A few grizzled travelers
- Mirages caused by the unrelenting heat
- Slight solace for those who know how to look

## Known Inhabitants

Few Badlanders live any part of their lives here. Those brave enough to dwell in the Big Nothing are by necessity nomadic — resources become exhausted easily and people must move on. Survival takes precedence over commerce.
