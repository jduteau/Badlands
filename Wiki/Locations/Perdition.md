# Perdition

> "Someone with a sense of humor, or someone who knew exactly what they were building."

---

## Overview

A small frontier town at the junction of two dry arroyos in the [[Wiki/Locations/Mil-Piedras]] canyon country. Named with either irony or self-awareness. Population is modest — maybe two dozen buildings total along a single main street of packed dirt.

---

## Description

The town sits at an arroyo junction below red sandstone buttes. Buildings are weathered, paint peeling, wood gray from sun and wind. Key establishments:

- **The Dusty Rose** — saloon run by [[Wiki/NPCs/Mae-Sutter]]; two stories with balcony; watered-down whiskey less than most; piano player who knows six songs; where the town does its talking
- **Miller's Livery** — run by [[Wiki/NPCs/Miller]]; weathered, hand-painted sign; solid tack room with a bolt lock; Miller sleeps in the loft
- **Aguilar's** — boarding house run by [[Wiki/NPCs/Mother-Aguilar]]; adobe two-story; clean; 50 cents/night, dollar with breakfast
- **General store** — sagging porch; men whittle outside
- **The jail** — where [[Wiki/NPCs/Tom-Blaine]] spends his mornings
- **White church** — steeple visible from the ridge, at the far end of town

Outsiders are measured carefully but not turned away. The town watches strangers with flat, practiced eyes.

---

## Current Status

Quiet. [[Wiki/NPCs/Tom-Blaine]] is investigating the murder of [[Wiki/NPCs/Eli-Crane]] and the theft of 40 cattle from [[Wiki/Locations/Harker-Ranch]], but progress has stalled. The town knows something is wrong north of here and is waiting to see what comes of it. [Session 1]
