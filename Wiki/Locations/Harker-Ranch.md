# Harker Ranch

A cattle ranch two days north-northeast of [[Wiki/Locations/Perdition]], reached by following the canyon out of town, taking the left fork at the dead juniper, and keeping the mesa on the right.

---

## Overview

A working cattle spread in the [[Wiki/Locations/Mil-Piedras]] canyon country. Run by [[Wiki/NPCs/Old-Man-Harker]], though in practice [[Wiki/NPCs/Clara-Harker]] manages the operation since Harker took ill. The ranch lost a hand and 40 head of prime cattle about ten days before Session 1.

---

## Description

A modest but solid operation:
- Weathered two-story main house with a wraparound porch
- Barn with horse corral
- Bunkhouse — long and low, built for a crew of 6–8; currently nearly empty
- Smokehouse, root cellar, chicken coop, kitchen garden, well
- Windmill

The brand is a **crooked H**. Small herds graze in the surrounding draws. The land opens up after the low rise south of the house.

---

## Current Status

Under-staffed and under threat. [[Wiki/NPCs/Old-Man-Harker]] is ill and querulous. [[Wiki/NPCs/Clara-Harker]] runs the operation alone with minimal hired help. Jake Powell hired on as a ranch hand at the end of Session 1. [Session 1]

The bunkhouse shows only 2 of 8 bunks in recent use. Eli Crane's bunk — corner, far end — has **E.C.** carved into the frame. [Session 1]
