# Back East

## Overview

The place on the map showing you where not to go. Back East embodies everything Badlanders' ancestors derided and left. Cities, pollution, fashion, and politics. You'd better have a damn good reason for heading here — you'll be as out of place there as Easterners would be in the Badlands.

## Description

- Bunch of haughty tinhorns
- New-fangled doodads and whatsits
- Pollution, congestion, and noise
- Ostentatiously tall buildings
- Fashion and other foolishness
- "Civilization"
- Odious responsibilities
- All sorts of people living cheek to jowl

## Known Inhabitants

People Back East make their living either by exploiting others or by being exploited. Because others grow their feed and protect their land, hundreds or thousands of them can have outlandish occupations like "financier" or "alderman."
