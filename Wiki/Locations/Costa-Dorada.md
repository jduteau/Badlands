# Costa Dorada

One of the most populous regions in the Badlands, home to the largest settlements. Beautiful sunlight gilds rolling grass-covered hills, majestic trees, golden beaches, and crystal-clear waters. Hot, dry summers and mild, wet winters yield a profusion of life. Costa Dorada sits on the western edge of the continent and draws people — and trouble — in equal measure.

## Features

- Warmth of radiant sunlight
- Crystalline waters
- The odor of blossoms on the breeze
- A plenitude of both flora and fauna
- Well-organized settlements
- All manner of entrepreneurs, legitimate and otherwise
- Cultures mixing and clashing
- Riches of all sorts and the dangers they bring

## Inhabitants & Culture

Folks make their livings in just about every possible manner, legal and not. The promise of easy riches — including gold in the hills — draws crowds and those willing to step out of line.

## Quest Starter

Your friend claims to have struck a gold motherlode. Others believe it too — why else would they apply such terrible pressure to give up the claim? Who is this prospector, and why are you willing to protect them and maybe their supposed claim?
