# Sea of Grass

## Overview

Undulating waves of green in every shade stretching from horizon to horizon. Gorgeous until it becomes hypnotic — some travelers crossing on foot have gone mad at its never-ending sameness. Those who approach it on its own terms find bounty. Moderate temperatures and rainfall make for great growing and grazing conditions. A whole ecosystem — from herds of wild bison down to the tiniest insects — depends on the grass.

## Description

- Every shade of green
- A long, low sky
- The unending susurration of the wind through grass
- Sturdy built houses jutting from the flat land
- Rich, black soil
- Practical people
- Well-fed herds, both tended and wild
- The rich odor of growth and decay

## Known Inhabitants

Ranching and homesteading are the primary occupations. Grass makes hay, and hay feeds cattle. Many folks heading west are more than content to set down roots in the same place that all that grass does.
