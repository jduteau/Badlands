# Tall Pines

## Overview

The most lush and verdant region in the Badlands. Forests of monumentally tall trees — pine and otherwise — blanket highlands, lowlands, slopes, and valleys. A moist, temperate climate with warm winters and mild summers supports vibrant green life from moss to redwood, along with a rich variety of fauna.

## Description

- Hushed, humid air
- Colossal trees
- Soft blanket of leaves and debris
- The skittering of unseen animals
- Cacophonous birdsong
- Rich scent of growth and decay
- Well-organized permanent communities

## Known Inhabitants

Badlanders here focus their energies on nature's bounty. Hunting, fishing, trapping, and forestry are lucrative activities. The bountiful land supports larger settlements where all types of folks band together.
