# High Plains

The wind rules here. In summer it offers the benediction of a gentle breeze; in winter, the scourge of howling fury. It leaves rich topsoil for nutritious crops or whips it away into the ether leaving only scrub and sagebrush. Towering plateaus and unbroken expanses of rock stretch at unceasing elevation under thin air that leaves outsiders gasping.

## Features

- Towering plateaus
- Unbroken expanses of rock
- Unceasing elevation
- Thin air that leaves outsiders gasping
- Well-adapted flora and fauna
- Beauty and desolation side by side
- Studies in contrast
- Stout, low-slung buildings
- The wind — always the wind

## Inhabitants & Culture

Every Badlander here takes the wind into account. Houses are built against its wrath. Mills harness its strength. Fields are planted to inhibit its erosion. Every decision must be made with the wind in mind — it is a constant, conspicuous presence even in its absence.

## Quest Starter

It's been days since the wind last blew. At first folks thought it a pleasant reprieve; now they try to laugh off their panic. Whispers as to why range from practical to occult. What do you fear is true? And how will you go about confirming — or, god willing, disproving — it?
